### ⚙️ RETRY AND CREDIT RULES

```
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

RETRY RULES:
────────────────────────────────────────────────
First attempt:   Full credits if passed
Second attempt:  50% credits
Third attempt+:  No credits (just practice)

MINIMUM SCORE TO EARN CREDITS:
────────────────────────────────────────────────
Score 1-4:   No credits (try again)
Score 5-6:   50% of template credits
Score 7-8:   Full template credits
Score 9-10:  Full credits + +20 bonus

EXAMPLE:
Template I1 (Professional Email) = +30 credits
Score 4/10 = 0 credits
Score 6/10 = +15 credits
```
